Terms & Conditions

Bookings
  • Bookings can be done through our FB or IG Page
  • Non-refundable deposit of ₱300 is required to secure a booking and will be deducted from balance payment of package availed
  • Bookings are accepted at least a day before the requested shoot
  • Booking request for 10AM of the same day is subject for approval. Otherwise you can walk in from 11am–5PM (except Tuesdays)
Payment
  • Balance is due at the end of the session. Accepted: BPI bank transfer and GCash Ewallet
Walk-in
  • Walk-in inquiries will be accommodated subject to availability, but will not be entitled to any promotions or discounts
Graduation Packages
  • Let us know what hood color you need for the shoot to check the availability
  • Please wear a black or nude tube top for the Filipiana shots for women, and white shirt with no collar for Barong shots for men
  • We provide pearl earrings and necklace for women. You can bring your own accessories too
Basic Hair & Make Up Services
  • Subject to availability
  • Please advise upon booking if you want to add this to your package unless stated otherwise
  • Please arrive 1 hour ahead with clean, dry hair and a makeup-free face
  • We recommend sending us your inspiration photos for your desired hair and makeup style
Discounts & Promotions
  • Discounts apply to the studio session package itself and do not include additional services such as makeup artistry and hair styling
  • Discounts and promotions cannot be combined with other promotions or discounts offered by the studio
  • The studio reserves the right to modify or cancel promotions at any time without prior notice
  • Rescheduling of existing bookings will not qualify for any promotions. Regular rates will be applied at the time of the rescheduled session
Equipment & Studio Use
  • Equipment: Clients are responsible for proper use. Any damage caused by misuse will be the client's responsibility
  • Studio Use: The studio may use client photos & recordings for promotional purposes, unless otherwise agreed upon
  • Event Terms and Conditions are given with agreed contract
